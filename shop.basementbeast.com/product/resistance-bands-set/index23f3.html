What makes Basement Beast Resistance Bands so unique?

We use a unique process called layered pressing when producing our bands.
Most resistance bands are made by pouring rubber into a single mold.
Basement Beast Bands are made by shaking our premium natural latex into multiple layers, eliminating air bubbles and other imperfections. From there, we press 3-12 (depending on band width) different layers together to from one solid and snap resistant band.
